About

Music with a purpose.

Sun of Red is a project built on the idea that music can do more than entertain. It can make us think, feel, and grow.

These songs explore history, reason, belief and compassion — not to give answers, but to invite reflection.

Genre: Indie Pop / Alternative

Themes: Truth, History, Humanism
